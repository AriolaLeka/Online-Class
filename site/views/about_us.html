About Us

This is a program which tries to create a class forum in which it will be possible
to communicate between the teacher and the students of the class.
The teacher will distribute lectures and students will have the opportunity
to comment on the lecture and normally respond to certain tasks.
Thank you for visiting our site, we always welcome you.
